How Are Invaders Generated?
New Invaders can be uncovered (minted) by anyone who owns PixelCons. Invaders are generated using existing PixelCons as a source of randomness and minted as PixelCons themselves.
More Info
What Are Invaders?
Invaders are space creatures that come from a different world. Each Invader belongs to a specific type indicating their planet of origin as well as elemental type.
Types
What Properties Do Invaders Have?
The level of an Invader is determined by the number of highlight pixels it has. Specific base colors also determine different attributes too. Certain properties are rarer than others.
