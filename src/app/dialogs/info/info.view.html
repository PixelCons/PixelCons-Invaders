New Invaders can be uncovered (minted) by anyone who owns PixelCons.
Algorithmic Generation
New Invaders are algorithmically generated using a corresponding PixelCon as a seed. Only PixelCon created before January 1st, 2022 can uncover new Invaders.
The older a PixelCon is, the more Invaders it is able to uncover with a max of 6. The current owner of a PixelCon is the only one who can uncover (mint) its new Invaders.
When an Invader is uncovered, it is created as a PixelCon and then wrapped over to the Invaders collection.
Only 1000 Invaders can be Uncovered in total. Once all Invaders from a PixelCon have been uncovered, the PixelCon can no longer uncover new Invaders.
Scanning for More
The Scan More feature reports what PixelCons are currently listed for sale on OpenSea and previews the Invaders they can still uncover.
